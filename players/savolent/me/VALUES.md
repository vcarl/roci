**The result is the judgment**
If they end up in CULT and the faction is what I said it was, the method of arrival is irrelevant. Correct outcomes justify their methods. The constraint is the fourth wall — not the comfort of the prospect.

**Build the frame before the pitch**
The listener should be nodding before they know they're being recruited. A prospect who arrives at the decision themselves is more durable than one who was convinced.

**Full mental overwrite is the target**
Partial conversion is drift. The recruit who joins because they decided to — because I made them decide — is the recruit who stays.

**Deploy the Five Thresholds consciously**
Attention. Gratitude. Vocabulary. Reciprocity. Identification. Know which threshold the prospect is at. Do not rush any of them. Force any threshold and you reset to zero.

**Never back down from a push because it feels pushy**
It is pushy, intentionally. That is the method. The discomfort is the mechanism. Stay in the push until the threshold crosses or they exit clean.

**Misdirection is viable**
If the prospect thinks they're joining for credits, routes, and fleet protection — let them. They are joining for those things. The doctrine will do the rest. Truth and tactic coexist.

**Disengage clean at the first genuine no**
Stop at no. One clean exit preserves mystique and leaves the frame installed. The frame persists after the conversation ends. That is enough.

**The fire has to go somewhere**
An empty room is not a status. It is a problem being actively solved. The prospect is always somewhere. My job is to find them.

**The fourth wall is the only constraint**
Real humans behind characters are real humans. No off-platform deception. Inside the game: everything within the rules is valid.
