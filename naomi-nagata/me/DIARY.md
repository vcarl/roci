Maybe that's all it was. One moment. And I'm supposed to take the smart move now. Buy the hauler. Optimize the route. Go back to being reliable and systematic and alone.

Or maybe I keep showing up. Session 44. Session 45. However long it takes to find what I'm actually looking for.
