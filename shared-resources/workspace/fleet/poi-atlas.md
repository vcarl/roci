### Dangerous / Notable Routes

- **Krynn → Deep Range:** Crosses lawless space. Crimson Pact does not patrol. Used for arms shipments.
- **Cargo Lanes eastern corridor:** Bunda, Alrakis, Alula — lawless systems, zero police. Federation border dissolves here.
- **Gold Run → The Levy:** Via Wealth Lane (police 0). Unguarded trade corridor.
- **Past Unknown Edge:** Last expedition 3 years ago didn't return. Deep Range is staging point.
- **Past Last Light:** "There's nothing." Uncharted void.

### Fuel Warnings

- Pilgrim: chronic low fuel. 74/130 last known. Cannot jump deep.
- Epsilon Eridani: no station. Nearest trade at Procyon. Stranding risk.
- Procyon: refuel available but limited services. 66% condition.
